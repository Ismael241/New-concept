A propos de nous

Luckely, Nataaosim knew where to go for a help. She found safety at the Kajiando African infant church boarding Primary school. Priscillia Nangurai, the principal has spent much of her life making sure that massai girls are allowed to get an education. She protected Nataaosim when her father and bridegroom came to school. « I told them that the girl is in my hands. » Says Nangurai. Nataaosim’s father shouted that if Nataaosim chose school over marriage, she was never to return home!

Last july Nataaosim Mako ran away from home. Her father told her she must drop shool to get married . « I did’nt want to marry » she said. » I wanted to stay to school» Nataaosim is only 10 years old . She belongs to Massai tribe, a nomadic people in Kenya. The massai value bravery intelligence and fairness but place little importance on education especially for girls. .
